Reinventing the radio request line

Spins.FM is reinventing the radio request line using social media. The app helps artists and radio stations engage fans by making it fun and easy to request songs on local radio - still the most proven way to drive demand for shows and increase sales. Artists using Spins.FM include Selena Gomez, John Legend, Zendaya, 50 Cent, Demi Lovato, Mac Miller, Britney Spears and many more. Since launching in New York City in 2010, Spins.FM has processed millions of social radio requests from music fans in the US, and is currently expanding internationally. Visit spins.fm or download the iPhone app.
